Shop Price Calculator

Dukaan waalon se bole gaye daam ko daalein — phir aap weight (gm/kg), quantity (piece/dozen) ya rupees dala kar turant nateeja dekhein.

Kaise kaam karta hai (short):
  • Base quantity + base unit + base price daaliye (example: 1 kg = ₹42 or 12 piece = ₹60).
  • Phir desired quantity/rupee dijiye (example: 50 g ya ₹10).
  • Calculator convert karke price ya quantity batata hai.

Responsive design — mobile/desktop dono pe chal jayega.
